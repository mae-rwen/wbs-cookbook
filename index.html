    • Ensalada Capresse

    A blend of Italian aroma.Buenisssssimo


    For the Salad

    • 450g fresh mozzarella
    • 680g vine-ripened tomatoes cored
    • 8g packed basi leaves ,tornor cut into thin strips
    • Balsamico-Cream two tb. spoon
    • Oliven oil 5-6 tb. spoon
    • 6g fine sea salt
    • Freshly ground black pepper

    Method of Preparation

    1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella in 5cm thick slices
    2. Arrange the salad on a serving platter,two to three slices of tomato for every piece of cheese
    3. Season with the salt the salt and pepper to taste
    4. Scatter the basil leaves over the top and drizzle with the oil and and balsamico-cream.
